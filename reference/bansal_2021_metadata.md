# Isolate metadata from Bansal et al. 2021

Isolate metadata from Bansal et al. 2021
(https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ygeno.2021.09.021) included for use as an
example data set. This is the parsed version of the included
"bansal_2021_metadata.tsv" file.

## Format

`data.frame`

## Source

Bansal, K., Kumar, S., Kaur, A., Singh, A. and Patil, P.B., 2021. Deep
phylo-taxono genomics reveals Xylella as a variant lineage of plant
associated Xanthomonas and supports their taxonomic reunification along
with Stenotrophomonas and Pseudoxanthomonas. Genomics, 113(6),
pp.3989-4003.
